MAU @ Michelin US5
PIV Operator Rules of the Road
• PIV inspections must be done at the start of every shift
   - Notify MAU Supervisor if any item knocks lift out of service

• Seatbelts MANDATORY at all times while operating

• DO NOT use monitor, I-Warehouse, or radios while moving
   - Lifts must be stationary when using equipment

• IMMEDIATELY notify MAU Supervisor of ANY incident or accident
   - Supervisor will notify Michelin personnel

• Always use three-point method for entering/exiting vehicles

• When parking a forklift, you must ALWAYS:
   - Lower the load
   - Set the parking brake
   - Place vehicle in Neutral
MAU @ Michelin US5
PIV Operator Rules of the Road (continued)
• Safe distance guidelines around pedestrians:
   - Minimum 10' if NO eye contact made
   - Minimum 6' if eye contact made
   - Minimum 3' even when speaking

• Operate only in approved aisleways
   - Never drive through pedestrian aisles or cut corners

• Maintain THREE-TRUCK-LENGTH distance when following

• NEVER pass another moving vehicle going same direction

• Use horn at: intersections, doorways, corners, congested areas, pedestrians

• Tipper lifts: NEVER tip or lower reel while moving - STOP FIRST

• DO NOT leave running lift unattended

• Tag out any PIV if lights, horn, or safety system is inoperative

• Check path behind (both shoulders) before reversing

• ONLY WATER allowed on lift | Keep forks 4" to 6" off floor

• High visibility t-shirts or vests REQUIRED
